Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a quote?
We typically provide initial quotes within 4-6 hours during business hours. For complex projects requiring detailed analysis, we aim to provide comprehensive proposals within 24 hours.
What information should I provide for a quote?
Please provide: Project type (audio/video), target audience, duration, language requirements, timeline, budget range, and any specific creative direction or examples you have in mind.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes! We offer 2 rounds of revisions for all projects to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions may be available depending on the project scope.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI, bank transfers, and digital payments. A 50% advance is required to start the project, with the balance due upon completion and approval.
Can you work with urgent deadlines?
Absolutely! We offer expedited services for urgent projects. Please mention your timeline in the project details, and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.
